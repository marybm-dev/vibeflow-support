VIBE FLOW

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Vibe Flow ("the app") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is important to us, and the app is designed to function entirely on-device.

Microphone Usage

The app requests access to your device microphone solely to analyze ambient audio frequencies in real time. This analysis drives the visual effects displayed on screen. No audio is recorded, saved, or transmitted at any time. All audio processing happens entirely on your device.

When using the video recording feature, you are given the explicit choice to include or exclude microphone audio. If you choose to include audio, it is captured only for the duration of the recording and saved locally to your device. It is never uploaded or shared by the app.

Data Collection

Vibe Flow does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No location data
  • No data is shared with third parties

In-App Purchases

Vibe Flow offers a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase status is stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Vibe Flow does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
