VIBE FLOW

Audio-Reactive Music Visualizer for iOS

About

Vibe Flow transforms music into real-time neon visuals. Play music from any app, and Vibe Flow uses your device microphone to analyze the audio and generate stunning procedural art that reacts to every beat, note, and rhythm. Mirror to Apple TV via AirPlay for a big-screen experience.

FAQ

How does Vibe Flow hear my music?

Vibe Flow uses your device microphone to pick up ambient audio. Play music from any app (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) and Vibe Flow will react to it. No audio is recorded or stored.

Does it work without music?

Yes. If you deny microphone access or there is no audio playing, Vibe Flow runs in demo mode with gentle organic pulsing so the visuals are never static.

How do I mirror to Apple TV?

Tap the AirPlay button in the app's HUD, or open Control Center and tap Screen Mirroring. Select your Apple TV and the visuals will appear on your TV.

What does Premium include?

A one-time purchase unlocks all 10 visual modes, Blend mode, all 5 color palettes, custom color picker, speed control, preset saving, auto-cycle, shake to randomize, and 60-second watermark-free video export.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap the screen to show the HUD, tap "Pro", then tap "Restore Purchase" on the upgrade screen.

Does Vibe Flow record my conversations?

No. Audio is analyzed in real time for frequency data only. Nothing is recorded, stored, or transmitted. When recording video, you are prompted to choose whether to include audio — if you decline, the video is silent.
